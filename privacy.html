Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 15, 2026

At Soul Syync, accessible from soulsyync.com, one of our main priorities is the privacy of our visitors. This Privacy Policy document contains types of information that is collected and recorded by Soul Syync and how we use it.

1. Information We Collect

If you choose to use our website to book a counselling/spiritual healing session or contact us, we may collect the following personal information:

  • Name
  • Email Address
  • Phone Number / WhatsApp Number
  • Preferred Date and Time for Sessions
  • Messages or description of needs you share with us

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect in various ways, including to:

  • Provide, operate, and maintain our website
  • Process your bookings and schedule sessions
  • Communicate with you (via email, phone, or WhatsApp) to confirm sessions or answer inquiries
  • Send you updates, confirmation notifications, and marketing communications (with your consent)
  • Improve, personalize, and expand our website services

3. Log Files

Soul Syync follows a standard procedure of using log files. These files log visitors when they visit websites. The information collected by log files includes internet protocol (IP) addresses, browser type, Internet Service Provider (ISP), date and time stamp, referring/exit pages, and possibly the number of clicks. These are not linked to any information that is personally identifiable.

4. Cookies and Web Beacons

Like any other website, Soul Syync uses "cookies". These cookies are used to store information including visitors' preferences, and the pages on the website that the visitor accessed or visited. The information is used to optimize the users' experience by customizing our web page content based on visitors' browser type and/or other information.

5. Third-Party Services & Payment Gateways

We may use third-party payment processors (such as Razorpay or Stripe) to handle payments on our behalf. These third parties have their own privacy policies in respect to the information we are required to provide to them for your purchase-related transactions. We recommend that you read their privacy policies so you can understand the manner in which your personal information will be handled by these providers.

6. Data Security

We use reasonable security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, loss, or disclosure. However, please remember that no method of transmission over the Internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure.

7. Consent

By using our website, you hereby consent to our Privacy Policy and agree to its terms.
