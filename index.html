About the Centre

Know About Soul Syync

Your healing journey begins with being truly heard.

At Soul Syync — a Counselling & Healing Centre founded in Vadodara in 2003 — we believe that every person carries within them the innate capacity for wholeness. Our approach combines the wisdom of ancient Vedic traditions with modern psychological understanding to support your mental, emotional, and spiritual wellbeing.

Whether you are navigating anxiety, grief, relationship challenges, or a deep desire to know yourself better, Jayshree Dixit and her team offer a safe, non-judgmental space where transformation begins.

About Your Guide

Meet Jayshree Dixit

With 22+ years of experience, Jayshree Dixit guides you toward emotional clarity, inner peace, and true self-discovery through holistic healing, personal counselling, and Vedic astrology.

"I believe that every person who walks through the door is already whole, they just need the right space to remember that. Healing is not something I do to you; it is something we discover together."

Jayshree's path to healing began with her own journey of self-discovery. Over two decades, she has counselled students, parents, families, and professionals, helping each person navigate life's most challenging chapters with clarity, compassion, and courage.
